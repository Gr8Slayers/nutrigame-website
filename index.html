About the Project

🍎

Nutrition Tracking

Detailed logging with precise portion calculations for macros, alongside dynamic weight and daily mood dashboards.

🏆

Gamified Progress

Stay motivated by maintaining daily streaks, joining community challenges, and unlocking unique achievement badges.

🤖

AI Fitness Coach

Engage with NutriBot, your personal AI dietitian, providing tailored diet plans, meal suggestions, and health Q&A.

📸

Smart Food Scanning

Take a photo of your meal, and our AI vision instantly analyzes its exact calories and nutritional values in seconds.

🌍

Social Community

Connect with friends, share detailed step-by-step recipes, and post workout moments to build a healthier ecosystem together.

⚡

Modern Architecture

Engineered with React Native, Node.js, and CockroachDB for a seamless, fast, and scalable cross-platform mobile experience.
